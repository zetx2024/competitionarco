WHAT IS IARCO?

The International Academic Research Competition is where talented students from around the world compete to present high-quality research on any topic.

IARCO challenges students to sharpen their critical thinking skills, immerse themselves in the research process, and hone their writing skills for success.

WHO CAN COMPETE:

High school and university students from around the world can participate in IARCO. The competition is open to both beginners and experienced researchers who are passionate about exploring academic topics.

WHERE WILL IT TAKE PLACE:

IARCO is conducted entirely online, allowing participants from any location to compete. All submissions, bootcamps, and evaluations are handled through our digital platform.

WHY YOU SHOULD JOIN

Boost your extracurriculars with an accredited research competition to stand out as a top applicant at your dream university.

Gain useful skills through our exclusive bootcamps with experts in the field and improve your research skills.

Whether you have lots of experience or are just starting out, IARCO is for you! All you need is a passion for research.

Win exclusive prizes: 25%-100% off renowned YRJ Research program with the opportunity to gain amazing cash prizes.

A great opportunity to fine-tune your research abilities and explore your interests before heading off to university.

RESEARCH TOPIC

IARCO is unique in that participants can research any topic they want!

This competition is not limited to the sciences. Your research topic can range from political science, art, and economics to engineering, history, or any other field of interest.

Researchers can choose a topic they're passionate about, something they wish to dive deeper into, or something they want to study in university.


HOW DOES THE COMPETITION WORK:

  • STEP 1

    IARCO is an individual research competition and is not open to group entries.

    After registration, provide answers to a few short questions about your research topic (Assessment Form).

    Find all submission dates & instructions here!

  • STEP 2

    Gain experience by participating in exclusive bootcamps on subjects like research proposal development and data analysis from experts in their fields.

    Submit your 1-2 page research proposal shortly after all bootcamps end.

    Find all submission dates & instructions here!

  • STEP 3

    The top 20 research proposals are chosen to advance to the next round. Each finalist films and submits a 2.5-5 minutes elevator pitch video.

    Winners are declared based on the quality of their research proposal and video.

    Find all submission dates & instructions here!
