What is IARCO?

The International Academic Research Competition (IARCO) is a prestigious global event where talented students from around the world compete to present high-quality, original research across all academic disciplines. Whether you're in STEM, humanities, social sciences, or arts, IARCO provides the perfect platform to showcase your intellectual curiosity and research skills.

Global Platform

Connect with researchers worldwide and gain international recognition for your work.

Diverse Disciplines

All academic fields welcome - from quantum physics to medieval poetry and everything between.

Prestigious Awards

Win scholarships, publication opportunities, and recognition from top academic institutions.

How Does the Competition Work?

1

Registration & Initial Submission

Register as an individual competitor and submit your research topic through our assessment form. This helps us understand your area of focus and prepare appropriate resources.

View submission dates and detailed instructions for this phase.

2

Research Development Phase

Participate in our exclusive bootcamps covering research methodology, data analysis, and academic writing. Then submit your 1-2 page research proposal demonstrating your project's significance and methodology.

Access all bootcamp schedules and preparation materials.

3

Final Presentation

Top proposals advance to the final round where you'll create a 2.5-5 minute video presentation explaining your research. Winners are selected based on proposal quality, presentation skills, and academic rigor.

Review the evaluation criteria for the final round.

Research Without Boundaries

IARCO stands out by allowing participants to explore any academic topic they're passionate about. Your research can span from nanotechnology to Renaissance art, from economic theories to AI ethics - if it can be researched academically, it's welcome at IARCO.

Choose a subject you're deeply curious about, something you want to investigate further, or a topic you might pursue in higher education. This is your opportunity to dive deep into what fascinates you most.
