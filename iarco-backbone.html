The IARCO Skeleton

The backbone of our International Academic Research Competition

Our Structure

The IARCO Skeleton represents the organizational framework that supports our competition. Just like a skeleton provides structure to the body, our team provides the foundation for this global academic initiative.

Comprised of distinguished academics, experienced researchers, and dedicated organizers, the IARCO Skeleton ensures the competition maintains its high standards of excellence and fairness.

Our Mission

We are committed to fostering academic excellence and research innovation among students worldwide. Our team works tirelessly to create opportunities for young researchers to showcase their work on an international platform.

Through mentorship, rigorous evaluation, and continuous improvement, we aim to make IARCO the premier student research competition in the world.

Meet Our Team

Our Organizational Structure

Advisory Board

Provides strategic guidance and ensures academic integrity of the competition. Comprised of senior academics and industry leaders.

IARCO Committee

The core organizing team responsible for competition operations, logistics, and day-to-day management.

Mentors

Subject matter experts who guide participants through the research process and provide valuable feedback.

Judges

Renowned experts who evaluate research submissions and provide constructive feedback to participants.

Guest Speakers

Renowned researchers and professionals who share their expertise through special sessions and workshops.

Ambassadors

Regional representatives who promote IARCO and support participants in their local areas.

Alumni Network

Former participants who contribute to the competition's growth and mentor new generations of researchers.
