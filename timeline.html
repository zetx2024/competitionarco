Competition Timeline

All important dates and deadlines for IARCO 2025

All deadlines presented are in Eastern Standard Time (EST)

Registration Period

May 1 - August 28, 2025

Early Bird Registration

  • Opens: May 1st at 00:00 EST
  • Closes: July 28th at 23:59 EST
  • Discounted registration fee available during this period

Regular Registration

  • Opens: August 1st at 00:00 EST
  • Closes: August 28 at 23:59 EST
  • Standard registration fee applies

Participant-Exclusive Bootcamps

September 10, 2025
  • Access opens: September 10th at 00:00 EST
  • Bootcamps are available on-demand, meaning participants can watch them whenever it is convenient for them starting on the above-mentioned date.
  • Although viewing the bootcamps is not mandatory, crucial information on how to conduct research and write an effective proposal will be provided and may therefore play a helpful role in how successful participants are in the competition.
  • Instructions on how to access bootcamps will be provided to participants via email in the days leading up to September 10th.
  • View bootcamp schedule and topics

Proposal Submission Deadline

September 30, 2025
  • Submit by: September 30 at 23:59 EST
  • The Research Proposal is the main research participants submit during the competition. The research proposal must be a maximum of 2 pages (excluding cover page, title, citations, and references).
  • Information on how to write a proposal and what to include will be provided in the bootcamps as well as further communication.
  • A submission link for the Proposal will be emailed to participants on September 05.
  • View proposal guidelines and requirements

Qualification Round Winners Announcement

October 8, 2025
  • Announced: October 8th by 23:59 EST
  • The top 100 of each category proposals will move on to the next round and will need to prepare a video submission as part of the Final Round.
  • Winners will be notified via email and announced on our website.

Video Pitch Submission Deadline

October 15, 2025
  • Submit by: October 15th by 23:59 EST
  • The top 100 Finalists must submit a 2.5-5 minute-long video submission.
  • The Video Pitch is a presentation with slides which should state the proposal's research question or objective and provide an overall introduction to the participant's research process and potential future research avenues.
  • View the guidelines for information on how the Video Pitch will be assessed.
  • Technical specifications and submission instructions will be provided to finalists.

Global Winners Announcement

October 25, 2025
  • Announced: October 25th by 23:59 EST
  • The top 3 Winners in each category are announced and win prizes!
  • Winners will be featured on our website and social media channels.
  • Prize distribution details will be communicated to winners via email.
  • View competition prizes
