What is IARCO?

Running through September and November, IARCO is more than just a contest—it's a guided immersion into the research process, designed for experts and newcomers alike!

For All Levels

Whether you're new to research or have experience, our competition is designed to challenge and inspire you.

Creative Freedom

Explore any research topic of your choice across STEM, Biology, MBBS, Humanities, Economics, and more.

Prestigious Recognition

Showcase your work to a global audience and compete for amazing prizes and opportunities.

Research Topics

STEM Fields

Explore cutting-edge research in Science, Technology, Engineering, Biology, and Mathematics. From quantum computing to biomedical breakthroughs.

Humanities

Dive into literature, philosophy, history, and cultural studies. Examine human experiences and societal developments.

Economics & Business

Investigate economic theories, market trends, business strategies, and financial innovations.
