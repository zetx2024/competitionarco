SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS

  • Cover page + 1-2 page research proposal + references (citations & references are not included in the 1-2 page proposal limit)
  • Any plagiarism detected will result in disqualification
  • No submissions will be accepted after the deadline

CITATION REQUIREMENTS

  • Datasets, publications, and other documentation must be properly cited
  • IEEE citations will be accepted
  • List the references in alphabetical order by the author's last name

FONT & STYLE REQUIREMENTS

  • Font should be Times New Roman -- font size 12
  • Margins of 1 inch (2.5 cm) all around
  • Single spaced
  • Adobe Acrobat (PDF) format only

COVER PAGE REQUIREMENTS

  • Full name as it appears on your registration
  • Research topic should be clearly stated
  • Email address you registered with
