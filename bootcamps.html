EXCLUSIVE BOOTCAMPS

Comprehensive research training for IARCO participants

1

What You'll Learn

Our bootcamps provide essential training in academic research methodology, proposal writing, and presentation skills specifically designed for competition participants.

These exclusive resources are available for free only to those who join IARCO. Access links and instructions will be provided to all participants.

2

How It Works

All bootcamp materials are available on-demand, meaning you can watch them whenever it's convenient for you starting from the access date.

While viewing the bootcamps is not mandatory, they contain crucial information that will help you conduct research and write an effective proposal for the competition.

Bootcamp Curriculum

1

Your First Step Into Academic Research

This foundational lesson introduces you to the world of academic research, covering the basic concepts and expectations for competition participants.

Topics Covered:

  • Understanding academic research standards
  • Selecting a research topic
  • Developing research questions
  • Ethical considerations in research
  • Introduction to literature review
2

Research Methodology

Learn about different research methodologies and how to select the most appropriate approach for your project.

Topics Covered:

  • Quantitative vs. qualitative research
  • Experimental design
  • Data collection methods
  • Sampling techniques
  • Ensuring validity and reliability
3

Research Proposal

Master the art of writing a compelling research proposal that clearly communicates your project's value and methodology.

Topics Covered:

  • Structure of a research proposal
  • Writing clear objectives
  • Literature review techniques
  • Methodology section
  • Expected outcomes and impact
4

Practice Research Proposal

Apply what you've learned by analyzing real research proposal examples and getting practical tips for your own submission.

Topics Covered:

  • Analyzing successful proposals
  • Common mistakes to avoid
  • Formatting and submission guidelines
  • Peer review process
  • Final checklist before submission
